About Our Company

Learn more about our legacy of strength, precision, and reliable supply chain.

Our Legacy

Established with a Vision of Strength

Founded in 2011, Shree Balaji Steel Enterprises started as a regional supplier of iron rods. Over fifteen years, we grew by securing direct distribution channels from premium mills. Today, we stand as a full-service construction materials partner supplying critical structural items, GI/MS pipe bundles, and cement to large-scale residential projects and industrial fabricators.

Operating out of our central Gandhidham inventory yard, we store high volumes of ready stock to minimize lead times. Our core philosophy stands on supplying genuine ISI-certified materials at wholesale rates.

"We build relationships that last as long as the structures built with our steel."

— Rajesh Kumar, Founder

Our Mission

To supply high-grade structural steel and core materials promptly and cost-effectively, acting as a reliable foundation block that engineers and developers can leverage to build safe and lasting infrastructure.

Our Vision

To scale our network to become the leading national distributor of industrial steel products, known for driving transparency in steel pricing, bulk fulfillment speeds, and supporting sustainable construction materials.

Milestones

Timeline of Growth

A look back at how we expanded our yard capabilities, brand relationships, and distribution networks.

2011

Foundation & Early Days

Shree Balaji Steel Enterprises was founded as a retail supplier of reinforcement steel rods in Gandhidham with a small local team.

2015

Piping & Structural Expansion

Secured partnership agreements with primary MS & GI pipe mills, increasing our capabilities to serve industrial piping grids.

2019

Modern Warehouse Yard Setup

Constructed a state-of-the-art storage yard spanning 50,000 sq.ft. to stack industrial structural beams, angles, and plates.

2026

Wholesale Regional Distributor

Serving hundreds of builders and retail dealers, utilizing our own transport fleet for direct site drops across the state.
