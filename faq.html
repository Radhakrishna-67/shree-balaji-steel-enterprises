Common Inquiries

Quick answers relating to bulk supply policies, logistics, and material certifications.

Yes. We routinely supply structural and reinforcement steel in bulk capacities (multi-ton loads) to major construction projects. For very large custom structural specifications or non-standard weights, we coordinate directly with primary mills to slot priority rolling schedules matching your target dates.

We supply structural products from premier Indian manufacturers including Tata Steel, JSW Steel, SAIL, Jindal Steel & Power, and regional rolling mills of repute. For cement, we stock OPC and PPC grades from brands like UltraTech, Ambuja, and ACC Cement.

Yes. We run a dedicated fleet of transport trailers and loading trucks. Depending on the volume of the order and site access, we arrange flatbeds equipped with hydraulic loading arms or truck transit direct to residential plots or commercial construction boundaries.

We accept Bank RTGS/NEFT transfers, Demand Drafts, Cheques, UPI, and major business credit/debit cards. For corporate buyers, government contractors, and registered sub-dealers, we extend credit limits subject to satisfactory reference vetting and trade volume history.

Absolutely. All structural steel and TMT bar shipments sent from our Gandhidham stockyards are backed by official Mill Test Certificates (MTC) matching the heat numbers cast on the steel. These document the exact tensile yield strength, elongation metrics, and chemical alloys.
